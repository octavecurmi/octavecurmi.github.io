About me

I am a postdoc of the Aix-Marseille Université, working mainly with the Singularity group.
I obtained my PhD in 2019 from Université de Lille, under the supervision of Patrick Popescu-Pampu.
You can find out more about me in my CV.

Research interests

Topological and Lipschitz aspects of Singularities, Toric and Tropical Geometry in singularity theory.
I am particularly interested in the connections between topological and metric aspects of the singularities of algebraic varieties, specifically through the study of Milnor fibers of smoothings.
